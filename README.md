# 📊 Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard — Power BI Project

This project analyzes customer churn using the **IBM Telco Customer Churn dataset**.  
The goal is to understand **why customers leave**, identify **high‑risk segments**, and uncover **actionable insights** for retention strategies.

---

## ⭐ 1. Project Overview
This dashboard provides a complete view of customer behavior, service usage, billing patterns, and churn trends.  
It is designed for business stakeholders to quickly identify churn drivers and make data‑driven decisions.

---

## ⭐ 2. Dataset
**Source:** IBM Telco Customer Churn Dataset  
**Rows:** 7,043 customers  

**Key fields include:**
- Customer demographics  
- Account information  
- Contract type  
- Internet service  
- Payment method  
- Monthly & total charges  
- Churn status (Yes/No)

---

## ⭐ 3. Key KPIs
- **Total Customers:** 7,043  
- **Churned Customers:** 1,869  
- **Churn Rate:** 26.54%  
- **Average Tenure:** 32.37 months  
- **Average Monthly Charges:** $64.76  

---

## ⭐ 4. Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers (Contract, Internet Service, Payment Method, Tenure)  
- Bar charts for customer distribution  
- Scatter plot for tenure vs monthly charges  
- KPI cards for quick insights  
- Clean, business‑ready layout  

---

## ⭐ 5. Insights
- Month‑to‑month customers churn the most  
- Fiber optic customers show higher churn  
- Electronic check users are the highest‑risk group  
- Customers with low tenure churn significantly more  
- Higher monthly charges correlate with churn
